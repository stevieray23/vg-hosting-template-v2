What EyeBreak is

EyeBreak is a free, open-source web app (a Progressive Web App) that reminds you to take short eye-rest breaks and guides a brief comfort exercise. It runs entirely in your browser, with no account and no server. It is provided for general screen-comfort purposes only.

EyeBreak is not medical advice and makes no claim to diagnose, treat, cure, prevent or improve any eye or vision condition. The breaks are about comfort and rest, not vision correction. If you have eye pain, vision changes, or any health concern, see a qualified professional. See the “Not medical advice” disclaimer at the bottom of every page.
