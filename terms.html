What EyeBreak is

EyeBreak is a free, open-source web app that reminds you to take short eye-rest breaks and guides a brief comfort exercise. It is provided for general screen-comfort purposes only. It is not medical advice. If you have eye pain or vision changes, see a qualified professional.
