How reminders work (honest version)
  • Tab open, visible: reminders appear right here, with a gentle chime — fully reliable.
  • Tab open, in background: you get a system notification (if allowed). Browsers throttle background tabs, so it can arrive up to a minute late.
  • Tab or app closed: no reminder is possible — a closed web app can't run. The next time you open EyeBreak, an overdue break is offered right away.
  • iPhone/iPad: notifications require iOS 16.4+ and installing via Share → Add to Home Screen. In a plain Safari tab they are not available.
  • Best setup: keep EyeBreak open in a pinned tab (desktop) or install it to your home screen (mobile).
