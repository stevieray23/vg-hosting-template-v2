Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-13

The short version

EyeBreak has no accounts, no ads, no tracking, no analytics, no cookies and no servers. We do not collect, transmit or sell any personal data. Everything the app remembers stays on your own device, and nothing is ever sent to us.

What is stored, and where

EyeBreak saves all of its state in your browser’s localStorage under a single key, eyebreak.v1. That key holds:

  • your reminder interval and (if you unlocked it) a custom interval,
  • sound on/off and the chosen chime pack,
  • the chosen color theme,
  • scheduling timestamps (when the next reminder is due, pause/snooze state),
  • your daily break and skip counts, day streak and the dates they belong to, and
  • if you bought the supporter unlock, your unlock code and the name on it.

All of this lives on your device only. Clearing your browser storage deletes it. Nothing in this key is transmitted anywhere.

Nothing is transmitted

The app makes no network requests to verify your unlock code — verification happens entirely on your device using offline cryptography (Web Crypto). The app does not phone home, and there is no backend that could receive your data.

Notifications

If you grant notification permission, reminders are shown by your own browser or operating system. EyeBreak uses no push server; every notification is generated locally on your device.

Payments

If you choose to buy the supporter unlock, the purchase happens on a third-party checkout operated by Lemon Squeezy, which acts as the Merchant of Record. Your payment data is handled solely by Lemon Squeezy under its own privacy policy, available at https://www.lemonsqueezy.com/privacy. EyeBreak itself never sees or stores your payment details.

GDPR / CCPA

Because EyeBreak holds no server-side data about you, there is nothing for us to access, export, correct or delete on your behalf, and nothing for us to “sell” or “share”. You remain in full control of the on-device data at all times through your browser’s storage settings. Any data related to your payment is held by Lemon Squeezy, and requests about that data should be directed to them.

Children

EyeBreak is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children. Because we collect no personal data, the app does not knowingly gather information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. The “last updated” date above reflects the latest version.
